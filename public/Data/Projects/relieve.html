Relieve

Overview

Immerse yourself in "Relieve," a captivating puzzle game meticulously crafted using Unity, C#, and Adobe Illustrator. Train your brain with challenging and enjoyable levels designed to relax and entertain.

Game Features

  • Engaging Gameplay: Tap your way through levels and guide the ball to its goal. Simple mechanics make it easy to pick up and play, while challenging levels test your skills.
  • Beautiful Art: Enjoy breathtaking, colorful graphics that elevate the visual experience and provide a sense of relaxation.
  • Relaxing Soundtrack: Unwind with a peaceful soundtrack designed to soothe your mind from daily routines.
  • Simple Characters: Unlock charming characters as you progress, each adding a touch of beauty to the game.
  • Cloud Save: Save your progress in the cloud to seamlessly continue your journey across devices, ensuring you never lose your achievements.
  • Offline Play: Enjoy unlimited offline gameplay at no cost, allowing you to play whenever and wherever you want.

Technologies Used: Unity, C#, Adobe Illustrator
