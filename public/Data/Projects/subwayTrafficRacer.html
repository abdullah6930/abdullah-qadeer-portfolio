Subway Traffic Racer

Overview

Dive into the adrenaline-pumping world of "Subway Traffic Racer," where you race against tiny cartoon cars in a thrilling subway traffic environment.

Game Features

  • Endless Racing Mode: Challenge yourself in an endless race through subway traffic, pushing the limits of your racing skills.
  • Realistic Sound and Stunning Locations: Immerse yourself in the game with authentic in-game sound and visually captivating environments.
  • Diverse Vehicles: Drive a variety of cartoonist blocky cars, trucks, and even jet planes, each with unique abilities and handling.
  • Power-Ups: Enhance your racing experience with jet plane power-ups to fly above traffic and magnet power-ups to collect gems effortlessly.
  • Upgrade and Customize: Use gem coins to buy new cars and upgrade them for enhanced performance in this exhilarating car game.
  • Competitive Gameplay: Compete globally, run as far as you can, and aim to beat high scores in this addictive racing experience.
  • Simple Controls: Easy-to-learn controls with slide or button options make it accessible yet challenging to master.

Technologies Used: Unity, C#
