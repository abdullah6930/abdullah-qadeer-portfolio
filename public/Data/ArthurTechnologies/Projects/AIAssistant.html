AI Assistant

Overview

At Arthur, I led the development of the Arthur AI Assistant, a groundbreaking tool designed to enhance collaboration in XR. This AI Assistant transforms meetings by acting as a versatile sidekick that not only answers questions but also organizes discussions in real-time. It adapts to any meeting scenario, whether as a visible presence in a virtual room or a private consultant, making it a dynamic participant in the collaborative process.

Mazars, a leading international firm, is trialing the AI Assistant to enhance productivity and engagement in their meetings. This innovative tool is set to revolutionize the way teams brainstorm, plan projects, and interact with clients, making meetings more efficient and interactive. Welcome to the future of meetings with the Arthur AI Assistant!

Features

  • Personal and Shared AI Assistant: Interact with the AI Assistant privately or with a single shared AI Assistant visible to everyone.
  • Speak to the Assistant: Typing usually slows down communication. Users can talk to the Assistant, and everything gets transcribed to text using Amazon Transcribe in real-time.
  • Get Instant Text and Audio Output: The response from the Assistant is displayed to users in the form of text, and instant audio is played through Amazon Polly.
  • Synced Audio Player Across Users: The audio is synced across users in real-time using Photon Server.
  • Audio Controls: Users can interact with previous Assistant responses, such as playing, pausing, or stopping the audio.
  • Speakerphone Mode: Users can toggle on Speakerphone mode, making the Assistant audible to everyone in the room despite the distance from the Assistant.
  • State Save: The shared AI Assistant history is saved on S3, useful for someone who wants to join the room in the future.

Technologies Used

Unity, .NET Core Server, ChatGPT API, Amazon Polly, Amazon Transcribe, and Amazon S3
