Just Catch Boxes

Overview

Designed, developed, and published independently, "Just Catch Boxes" is a mobile game that showcases my full-stack skills. From character design using Adobe Illustrator to programming and publishing on the Play Store, every aspect of this project reflects my dedication and creativity.

Game Features

  • Offline Play: Enjoy seamless gameplay without requiring an internet connection.
  • Unique Characters: Play as different characters like Penguin, Robot, and more, each meticulously designed by me.
  • Diverse Themes: Immerse yourself in various seasonal themes such as Summer, Winter, and Autumn, all crafted to enhance the player experience.
  • Missions and Rewards: Complete challenging missions to earn rewards and unlock upgrades, adding depth to the gameplay.
  • Social Integration: Connect with friends, compete on leaderboards, and earn achievements on Google Play, fostering a vibrant community around the game.

Technologies Used: Unity, C#, Adobe Illustrator
